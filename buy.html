The Purple Night

This mesmerizing painting captures a serene night scene dominated by tranquil shades of blue. A detailed full moon commands the center, casting a reflective path on calm water. Silhouetted trees frame the composition symmetrically, creating a tranquil lunar landscape. Varying blue strokes convey movement, adding to the overall sense of mystery and tranquility.

Smokey Night

The painting is a depiction of life. It shows that life is hard for some and easy for some. It can be choking for some and a care-free ride for others. It doesn't matter where you are in life, one must always learn to look at the bright side. To the bird in the cage, the world can be free or in a cage. To the bird outside, the world can be big and terrifying or an open sea of wonders waiting to be seen and felt.
